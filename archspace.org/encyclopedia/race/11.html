 The Trabotulins were an ancient race that existed long before the Empire was conceived. Their civilization flourished in the era of peace, and they achieved technological marvels that would have otherwise been unachievable in times of war. However, it is their technological aspirations that inevitably led to the creation of a weapon so powerful that brought their once glorious civilization to an abrupt end. The remnants of this once great race of people have since then dedicated themselves to the advancement of temporal sciences, in hope that one day they will be able travel back in time to resurrect their long lost heritage.
