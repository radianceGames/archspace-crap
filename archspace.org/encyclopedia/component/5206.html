Self-Conscious Computer(#5206)
 
 Category  Computer  Level  6
 Technology Prerequisite(s)  Self-Conscious Artificial Intelligence
 Project Prerequisite(s)  NONE
 Race Prerequisite(s)  ALL RACES
 Attack Rating  333  Defense Rating  616
 
 This is the sixth level of computer that has ever been seen on ships in the Empire. It is capable of non-heuristic-based data generation, which means that unlike its predecessors, it is capable of making optimal decisions in situations that it has never even encountered before. As such, scientists termed this level of computing 'self-conscious' - making ships equipped with such devices capable of being piloted without a crew.
