I lost fleet battle again, and I don't understand why. What factors decide the winner and loser?
The winner is decided by fleets remaining. If both sides have fleets left, it is a draw. Otherwise, whoever has fleets left wins. This does not include fleets which retreat from the battle.
What's the benefits of being a council's speaker?
The council speaker is allowed access to the council speaker page. He thus has the final decision on anything that the council does.
How can I delete obsolete ship designs?
If you look at the list of designs on the ship design page, you will see a delete button next to each design.
Which race is most powerful? or, Which race is easy to start with?
Hopefully each of the races is well balanced for strength. As to ease, you may wish to choose a race without any negative aspects.
What is a cluster?
A cluster is basically a group of stars that are near each other.
How can I launch an attack at other players?
You can attack anyone within your power range through the use of the war submenus. The only limit to this is that you must be at war with someone to siege them.

How can I speed up ship building time?

The race I choose seems too weak, so I want to change my race. How can I do it?
The only way to change your race is when the server is reset. For this reason it is important to choose your race wisely. Even when you die, if you return to the game directly, you begin with the same race. If you want to change your race, you must quit, and reregister your account.

What is council and What does a council do?
A council is a group of players. You may work together, or you may fight each other. Most of the interaction with the Empire is done thru council channels, and if you wish to declare war with anyone who is not in your council, you must have both of your councils go to war.

How can I play this game?
There is a basic guide for beginners. This guide explains the controls, and gives step-by-step instructions for many actions you will use in the game.
What is control model?
Control model is an index to how good you are at a certain aspect of technology, military, etc.
How can I get more planets?
There are two ways you can gain planets. You can send a fleet on an expedition, or you can declare war and siege someone to take one of their planets.
How can I modify fleets' formation?
If you use the defense plan page, you may modify the plans for defense. Your fleets use the generic defense plan, unless there is a special case plan that fits the situation. For offense, you will be able to choose your formations at the beginning of each battle.
What is Defense Plan?
A defense plan is the orders that your fleets will use when they are attempting to defend your planet.
I want to research any specific tech.
To research a specific tech, you should click on its name to see the description. Then, you should check the box and click okay. This will select the tech as the one you are currently researching.
What tech should I research?
This is a choice that is completely up to you. Each person on an individual basis will decide this.
