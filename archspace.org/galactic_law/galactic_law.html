The Empire will deal Severely with any who Violate these Strictures.
Any violators will be subject to immediate deletion. Repeated offenses may resulted in a server ban.
 
THERE SHALL BE NO MULTIPLAYING
Multiplaying is strictly prohibited. You may only have one account at any given time. (You can restart a new one if your character is destroyed or ceases to exist).
YOU MUST BE HONEST WHEN REGISTERING YOUR DOMINION
We require you to enter real personal information such as real name and email address when you create an account. If you are accused of cheating and the investigation finds a questionable name, your account will be subject to immediate deletion.
YOU RETAIN SOLE RESPONSIBILITY FOR ALL DEEDS IN YOUR SYSTEM
You shall bear responsibility for everything that happens in your account. Sharing accounts or trading or inheriting or taking care of someone else account is not permitted. Any questionable actions will be charged towards the account owner.
THERE SHALL BE NO HARRASSMENT OF OTHER PLAYERS
Harassing other players is strictly prohibited. It includes, but not limited to, using offensive language or sexually explicit language in messages or board, spam, or sending unwanted messages when told to stop. Please mind your attitude and language. Be polite and have fun. Taunting opponents is fine as long as it is role played. But insults or personal attack will not be tolerated, while aggressive behavior will be frowned upon. Please be considerate.
THERE WILL BE NO PASSING OF INFORMATION OUTSIDE OF PROPER CHANNELS
Do not post specific information about any player other than yourself that cannot otherwise be found by anyone without the use of an attack or covert operations. This includes detailed battle results, even though you may have been involved in the battle, you are revealing the other persons troop types and tactics. This also includes battle results that have resulted in the death of the other player. If you want to reveal such information to your allies (or council members), use the message system provided.
YOU MAY ONLY COORDINATE YOUR ACTIONS WITH THOSE RELATED TO YOUR POWER STRUCTURE
You may only coordinate attacks with people who are related to your power structure. This includes allies, council members, and any subjugated or dominating council members.
THERE WILL BE NO ABUSE OF BUGS
You must report any bugs found to developers. Taking advantage of any bug is not permitted.
HACKERS WILL NOT BE TOLERATED
Hacking is strictly prohibited, which includes any attempt to hack into other players account or the server.
YOU MUST READ ALL EDUCATION MATERIAL PROVIDED
Before you post anything on the boards or ask for help, please take advantage of FAQ and help files provided. The customer support will generally be cooperative and answer most questions. But requests for any type of reimbursement or to reveal player or game stats will be ignored ( so do not bother to ask ).
