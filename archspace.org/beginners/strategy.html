STRATEGY BASICS
 
  Strategy basicsl instructions:
To use this manual, select the topic from the list to the left. Seen below the Domestic Management page is used as an example.

Domestic Management:

-Concentration Mode:
For beginning, it is suggested that you use either balanced or research as your concentration mode. This is because to begin you will want PP to invest in improving your planets. Also, as you gain technologies, you may be able to purchase projects which will improve your overall control models.

-Planet Management:
There are two main things you can control on your growing planets. The first is the ratio in which buildings are built. This is important as you cannot later destroy buildings and rebuild them. For this reason it is suggested that you may want at least a small percentage of each type of building. The second thing you can do is to invest money into the development of the planet. This will usually increase terraforming speed, and it can sometimes boost population temporarily over the maximum. This population effect can be important, as your population determines, in part, the speed that things occur at.

-Research:
There are a couple things that you need to keep in mind when you decide if you want to do free research or if you will focus on one area. If you use free research there is a large bonus to the research speed. This can be quite an advantage unless you have a particular area you with to work on. For the first time, it may be best to use free research until you know which technologies are needed for certain benefits. Of the four areas of research, social helps most with diplomacy and council matters. It is important for the council speaker to have a good amount of social techs known. Life science is important for your planets environment and growth. Matter-energy science is where the majority of the ship components are learned. Information science helps with research speed. All of these areas are important, so it is often best to begin with free research until you reach higher levels and have greater knowledge of what you need. Another important aspect is investment. If you are actively investing money in research, you can reduce the time needed for research a lot.

-Projects:
As projects become available, it is important that you consider long range plans before you buy them all. Most have some sort of drawback and most projects cause permanent changes. For example, if you wish to focus later in the game on military, it may be best to not purchase some projects earlier in the game.
