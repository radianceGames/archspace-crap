STEP BY STEP
 
  6-1 Diplomacy  
The Diplomacy section is relatively simple. You can interact with the other players in your council. There are four major sections.
The first is simply a listing of your relations with the other players in your council.
On the status list page, you should decide how you will deal with the specific members of your council. Then move on to the Read Message section to see if you have any messages incoming.
The jagged line represents a message is needed to be replied.
The straight line represents a message that does not need a reply.
Step 1 (Read message): If you have a message, you should read it, and
                                         respond. To read a message, click on Read.
Step 2 (Read message): To respond to a message, you should choose
                                         one of the three choices and add any
                                         comments that you want. Then click Send.
Step 1 (Send message): If you wish to send a message of war or peace
                                         you must use the Send Message page. First
                                         you must select the person you wish to send
                                         the message. To do that, you should choose
                                         their name in the drop down menu box. Then
                                         click OK.
Step 2 (Send message): Now you should choose which type of message
                                         to send to this player. Once you have done
                                         that, you may type any comments you wish to
                                         add. Then click Send to send the message.
If you wish to learn more information about a player, you may use the Inspect Other Player page. To use this page, simply choose their name from the drop down menu, or enter their number in the open field. Then click okay.
