CATEGORY CONTROLS
 
 

Category control instructions:
To use this manual, select the topic from the list to the left. Seen below the Main page is used as an example.

Main:

This category is not that large, but it is quite important. This is where your status changes are listed. Any changes to your planets and any major news about your fleets or planets is listed here. You can also gain some basic information on your current status.

The following information is listed:
        The amount resources or production points (PP) you have stored
        The number of planets that you have
        The total population of your planets
             Note: Each population unit listed stands for 1000 people.
             That is what the "k" stands for
        The number of fleets you have
        The number of ships you have
        Your current honor ranking
        The name of your council
        The time until the next turn occurs.
