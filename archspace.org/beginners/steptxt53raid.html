STEP BY STEP
 
  5-3 Raid  
 
Step 1: Select Raid under the War category.
A raid is when you attempt to sneak one of your fleets past your enemy, and bombard their planet. There is no tactical battle for a raid. If you are caught, you do not have enough time to give detailed orders.
Step 2: First you must choose your target. You do not need to be at war
             with a person to raid them. After selecting your target,you should
             press Okay.
Step 3: You must now choose which fleet you will send. You are only
             allowed to send one fleet. After choosing, you should press OK.
Step 4: The final choice you make is choosing which planet to raid. Once
             this is done, you can click Attack to begin the raid.
