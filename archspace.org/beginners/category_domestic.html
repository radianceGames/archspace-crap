CATEGORY CONTROLS
 
  Domestic:

This category has its central page, and 5 sub links. The Domestic central page lists current domestic information.
        It gives the following information:
        The name of your home planet
        Your total number of planets
        Your current Concentration mode
        Your total population
        Your Control Model
        Your current number of each type of building
        The Projects that you have purchased that are in effect
        The total number of each Tech type that you have researched
        The Technology you are currently in the process of researching

The following sub links are also available at the bottom of this page:
        Concentration Mode
        Planet Management
        Research
        Project
        Whitebook

-Concentration Mode:
This link take you to a page where you can change your concentration mode. Your concentration mode decides what the majority of your efforts are placed into. There are four choices available:
        Balanced-you put equal effort into all areas.
        Production-you focus on producing resources.
        Military-you focus on producing ships and supporting your fleets
        Research-you focus on researching technologies.

-Planet Management:
This link brings you to a page where you can view basic statistics from all of your individual planets. You can see the name, number of people, and number of buildings for each planet.
        The name of each planet is a link to manage that particular planet.
        There are two different things you can directly manipulate on this
        page: you can set the future building plans for the planet and you
        can invest money in the terraforming of this planet.

-Research:
This link shows the main research interface. From this page you can see what technologies you are currently able to research. You can click on any tech for a description. On the description page there is a check box. If the box is checked, then you are focusing on gaining that technology. The other thing you can do on the research page is invest PP. This will increase the speed of your research.

-Project:
This link leads to a page where you will see a listing of the current projects you are able to purchase. You can purchase them using this page if you have enough PP in your bank.

-Whitebook:
This link takes you to a page where you can view the statistics of your domain in a different way. The events that are effecting your domain are also listed on this page. The information given directly on this page is the following:
        Character name
        Total PP
        PP income from the Previous Turn
        Population
        Concentration Mode
        Council
        Position (this is the position you have in the council)

There is also some other information on this page which is linked to additional pages. This information is:
        Planet Status:
        This lists your total planets and is linked to your planet
        management page.

        Research Status:
        This lists the total number of techs that you have researched, and
        also lists the current tech you are working on. This is linked to
         your research page.

        Fleet Status:
        This lists your total number of fleets and is linked to your central
        fleet page.

        Commander Status:
        This lists your total number of commanders and is linked to your
        fleet commander page.

        Diplomacy Status:
        This tells you how many new messages you have waiting for you
        and links to the central Diplomacy page.

        Control Model:
        This links to the control model page where you can view your
        relative skill in different areas and also the factors that effect each
        CM.
