  2-3 Concentration Mode  
 
On the domestic page you can see a listing of your total income for each resource. You can also see specific listings for what the income is spent on. Population and the number of buildings of each particular type determine the income. The Control Model for that resource further modifies this. You can alter CMs through certain technologies, projects, investment, and concentration mode.
Step 1: Use the side bar to move to the Concentration Mode page under             the Domestic category.
Step 2: On this page there is an explanation of the different concentration
            modes. If you use this you can see what changes will be made to
            your CM if you change concentration mode. Select the mode and
            click Done.
