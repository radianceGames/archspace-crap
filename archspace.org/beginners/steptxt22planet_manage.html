STEP BY STEP
 
  2-2 Planet Management  
 
Step 1: Click on 'Planet Management' to gain more information on your              planets and manage them.
Step 2: Click on the name of the planet that you wish to manage.
Step 3: At this point, you may wish to invest in your planet. If the
             Environment is less than suitable, investing will speed the rate of
             terraforming. To invest, enter an amount of PP into the space
             provided. Then click on OK.
You can change the building ratio or invest to your planet in any order. The order is not very important, but anyway the investment will speed up the building.

Facility Building Ratio:
  This is the ratio at which future buildings will be built on this planet. You   can increase the ratio by clicking on the '>>' sign or decrease by the '<<'
  sign. The total will be 100%. You can lock the ratio of one type of
  buildings by clicking on the lock icon. Then the icon will be changed to a
  locked one. Note: This ratio does not modify the buildings already in
  place.
Planet Management Glossary:

Population and Population Max:
  These tell you how many people can fit on this planet. This can change
  with certain projects or thru terraforming.
Production per Turn:
  This shows the initial Production Points generated by this planet.
Upkeep:
  This shows the upkeep required by the buildings of this planet.
Environment:
  This gives an indication of how this planet fits your species. If it is
  suitable, you do not need to terraform.
Size, Temperature, Atmosphere, Gravity:
  These are all factors that can your species interaction with the
  environment. These can be changed through terraforming.
Waste Rate:
  This shows the amount of production from this planet that is lost to
  waste. This will be higher for new planets unless you have certain
  projects which can reduce waste.
Attribute:
  This is for any extraordinary things that affect the area around this planet.
  Some of these will alter production or environment.
