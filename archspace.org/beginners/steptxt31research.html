STEP BY STEP
 
  3-1 Research  
 
Step 1: Use the side bar menu to move to the Research screen.
To increase your Research CM and research speed, you can invest PP. To do this, enter an amount of PP here and click OK.
Decide if you prefer Free Research or directed research. With free research, you have no choice of what is learned, but research proceeds faster. With directed research you choose a specific technology to research out of those you have available.
Free Research is the default, and you switch to Free Research whenever you have completed learning a specific technology with directed research.
Step 2: To use directed research you must select a technology to              research. To view information on each technology, click on its
             name.
Step 3: The discription page for each technology shows the effects and
             the cost of the technology. It also has a description of the
             technology. To select this technology for directed research, check
             the box and click on Done.
