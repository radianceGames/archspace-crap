STEP BY STEP
 
  4-1 Ship Design  
 
Step 1: Select Ship Design under the Fleet category.
A major part of Archspace is design of fleets and combat. There are three major parts to creating a fleet. The first is ship design. The second is ship building. The third part is Fleet formation.
Step 2: Ship Design is the first think you must do to prepare a fleet. The
             first step is to name the ship class that you are designing, and to
             choose a ship size class. Once you have done this, click OK.
Step 3: On the second page you must choose three types of things. First,
             you must choose your armor in the first drop down box. Then,
             you must choose weapons from the second set. Finally, if the ship
             class you have chosen has room for them, you should choose
             devices. After choosing all of these, you should click OK. This
             will take you to the next screen to see a preview of the design.
Step 4: Now you have a chance to look at the design preview. You
             should make sure all of your choices were entered correctly. If
             you like the design, you should press Register This Ship to save
             the design.
