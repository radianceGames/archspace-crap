CATEGORY CONTROLS
 
  Fleet:

This category has a central page and nine sub links. The Fleet central page has a listing of all the ships you have available that are not in any fleets. It also lists the status of all your current fleets and gives an option for disbanding them. Finally it contains the following sub links:
        Ship Design
        Ship Building
        Fleet Commander
        Ship Pool
        Form a New Fleet
        Reassignment
        Mission
        Recall
        Disband

-Ship Design:
This page is where you begin the design process of your fleets. Here you choose which class type you would like to design, and you name the new design. You can also view the basic statistics of the ship classes and your previous designs. There is also an option to delete previous designs that are obsolete.

-Ship Building:
This page is where you can set ships up in a queue to be built. You may also invest PP into ship building. The following information is also given on this page:
    The amount of your income which is used each turn for ship building
    The PP currently in your ship production pool. (progress on 1 ship)     The current amount you have remaining of your investment.
    The amount of the investment that is used each turn.

-Fleet Commander:
This page is where you can find information on your commanders. You can see which ones are assigned to fleets, and also their level, exp, the number of ships they can command, and their efficiency. There is also a checkbox you can use to dismiss unwanted commanders. The names of the commanders are also links to pages with more details on their skills.

-Ship Pool:
This page has three main sections. The first is your ship pool. This is the area where ships that are not in fleets are kept. The second section is the Repair bay. This is where ships go after combat if they have survived damage. The final section is the class specifications and is the same as the design lists found on the design page. You have the option of scrapping any ships that are in your pool for resources.

-Form a Fleet:
This page is where you form your fleets. You must give them a number, a name, and choose the number of ships and commander. There is also a brief description of all current fleets you have.

-Reassignment:
This page is where you go to modify your fleets. There are three options available:
         You can change the commander of the fleet
         You can change the ship type (Reassignment)
         You can remove/ or add ships

-Expedition:
You send a fleet out to look for new planets.

-Mission:
This page is where you can assign your fleets to different activities. There are five different missions you can send your fleets on:
        Train- This increases their experience.
        Station on Planet- This will place a fleet on a planet to try to
        detect pirates.
        Patrol- This is similar to Stationing, except it is only for 24 hours.
        Expedition- You send a fleet out to look for new planets.
        Dispatch to Ally- You send a fleet to help defend an allies planet.

-Recall:
This page is where you can recall fleets that have been sent on certain types of missions.

-Disband:
This page can be used to disband your fleets. Disbanded ships and commanders return to their respective pools.
