STEP BY STEP
 
  5-1 Siege Planet  
 
War is very important in Archspace. One of the main ways to gain planets is through sieges. Or, you can use it as a way to cripple your enemy economically. The war main page shows information that will often be useful.
The first list is a list of those you are at war with. This is only those you are officially at war with, not those you just harass. This list is important, as you can only siege an enemy if you have declared war.
The second list is a list of your recent battle reports. You can follow the links in this list to see the movie of the battle. This can aid you in planning future strategies.
Step 1: Select Siege planet under the War category. This takes you to the
             Siege Planet Page
If you wish to take a planet from an enemy, you must siege them. To Siege someone, you must first be at war with them. You can declare war with people in your council using the Diplomacy menu. Your council speaker may also declare war with other councils.
Step 2: You must first choose which target you wish to attack. Then click
             Okay.
Step 3: On this page you must now decide which ships you will send to
             the battle. You may have up to 20 fleets in each battle.
Step 4: You must also choose which ship will be your Capital ship. The
             commander of this ship will be the Armada Commander for the
             fleet. Once you have chosen, click "OK" to go to the next page.
Step 5: Now you must place your fleets and give them battle orders. This
             will determine their starting position and the tactics that they will
             use.
Step 6: The final choice to make is which planet you wish to attempt to
             take. After making this choice, you should press Attack. Your
             fleets will then begin the attack.
