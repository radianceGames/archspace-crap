The Agerus

    The Agerus are a militaristic race of giant, planet sized creatures that float through space. They get a plus four to military, which helps them produce bigger ships earlier. With the lack of commerce and the obvious lack of diplomacy, this is the key to their success. Getting planets and getting them fast is the best way to play Agerus. This offsets the lack of commerce and gets them the PP they need  and the extra military production that helps them fuel further expansion. Their abilities are ideal for this strategy (or will be once implemented). First, the no spying helps keep commanders form being assassinated, which helps make up for the low commander survival in battle. The bad part is that they can't spy either (it is kind of hard for a huge, ugly bug to spy effectively). Stealth, I imagine, will make it much easier to win battles because your ships will get to fire first, which is very important considering that a homing black hole can take out a whole mess of ships before the battle even gets heated. I'm not sure on the asteroid management, but if I had to guess, I would say that it increases production or efficiency to balance out the lack of commerce. Their innate tech isn't all that great, but with the quick buildup of planets, this shouldn't be a problem.
